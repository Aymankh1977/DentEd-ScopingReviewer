# PRISMA-ScR 22-Item Checklist

Source: Tricco AC et al. PRISMA Extension for Scoping Reviews
(PRISMA-ScR): Checklist and Explanation.
*Ann Intern Med.* 2018;169(7):467-473.

## Title
1. **Title** — Identify the report as a scoping review.

## Abstract
2. **Structured summary** — Provide structured summary including
   background, objectives, eligibility criteria, sources, charting
   methods, results, conclusions.

## Introduction
3. **Rationale** — Why the review is being conducted.
4. **Objectives** — Explicit statement of questions and objectives.

## Methods
5. **Protocol & registration** — Indicate whether protocol exists.
6. **Eligibility criteria** — Characteristics of sources of evidence
   used as criteria for eligibility.
7. **Information sources** — All sources searched and date of last search.
8. **Search** — Full electronic search strategy for at least one database.
9. **Selection of sources of evidence** — Process for selecting sources.
10. **Data charting process** — Methods of charting data from sources.
11. **Data items** — Variables for which data were sought.
12. **Critical appraisal** — (Optional) Methods used for critical appraisal.
13. **Synthesis of results** — Methods of handling and summarising data.

## Results
14. **Selection of sources** — Numbers screened, assessed for eligibility,
    and included; reasons for exclusions. (PRISMA flow diagram.)
15. **Characteristics of sources** — Characteristics of each source.
16. **Critical appraisal within sources** — (If conducted.)
17. **Results of individual sources** — Relevant data from each source.
18. **Synthesis of results** — Summary of results that align with
    review objectives.

## Discussion
19. **Summary of evidence** — Main results relative to objectives,
    in context of the literature.
20. **Limitations** — Limitations of the scoping review process.
21. **Conclusions** — General interpretation; implications for practice,
    policy, research.

## Funding
22. **Funding** — Sources of funding for the review and the role of funders.
